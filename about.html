About Me

I'm quietly confident and always aiming to improve on my work.

Hi, my name is Nkau Yer Lor but you can simply call me Yer. I will be graduating this Spring 2021 with my Bachelor of Science in Human-Technology Interaction, minor in Business at the University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point. I began my journey with my first internship right on campus in June 2019, then transitioned to accepting an intern position at Herrschners a year later. I've enjoyed all the work that I've done whether it was building wireframes, maintaining sites, creating graphics, digging into the codes and modifying them, etc.

I chose this field because I admire the technology and design aspects. Technology has been evolving tremendously and there are always new designs, trends, techniques, and that's what interests me about this field. To be able to keep up and learn all of these is never enough because it's always growing. I want to take part in a developing and design field. I specifically chose Human-Technology Interaction because with everything evolving, there are new ways to use new products, and being part of making a product easier for users to use is a way for me to give back.

What I'm excited about this career path are all the people I'll meet and techniques I'll learn. I think that it's incredible how other designers and developers go through the many stages of planning, developing, and testing to get to the final end product. There is so much creativity and passion involved.

Aside from all of this, I enjoy doing many other things! I have a passion for playing volleyball and have placed in the top three two times at Hmong Volleyball Tournaments. Volleyball helps me stay active and I enjoy meeting new players. I also enjoy hiking. This year, I made a goal for myself to enjoy nature more and hiking is the perfect way to achieve that goal! Lastly, once quarantine hit, I started doing digital illustrations and loved doing that! Check out some of my digital illstrations here :)

Positions that I am interested in range from UX/UI Designer to Web Design/Developer.
