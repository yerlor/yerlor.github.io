AquaBid - Visual Improvement

This course work shows a visual improvement of a current website. AquaBid isn't a very friendly website. The goal of this work was to find visual improvements and create a wireframe to where I would make suggestions.

Original Site: AquaBid website

Wireframe: Wireframe for improvement

Tool Used: Balsamiq

    Suggested Improvements:
  • Combined the account and registration links to just “Registration” & “My Account”
    This was done to improve the aspect of visual design because, from the original website, all the links that had to do with accounts were placed on the left side margin and navigation bar. By the improvement of placing them under two things keeps the design more simple and helps the user more rather than them having to keep looking at the margin to change any necessary information.

  • Moved the left side margin to the top of the page
    We moved the “new items, hot items, ending soon, etc…” to the top of the page and enlarged that. We felt that improving this aspect was important since when users get on the website, they would like to see what’s trending.

  • Created a left side margin for only necessary content
    Out of all the content from the original margin, we left the bids, track auctions, and feedback. This improves the design because it doesn’t have unnecessary content like the original one that doesn’t relate to one another

  • Added photos and dropdown boxes for the main content
    This improves the design because it grasped the user’s attention with live images and categorizes each topic. Under that, we added a dropdown box for each category for easier search. Ideally, it’ll be in alphabetical order.
